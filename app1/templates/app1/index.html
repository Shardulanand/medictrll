How does MediCtrl ensure good medical quality?

All MediCtrl hospitals are accredited by National Accreditation Board for Hospitals & Healthcare Providers (NABH), dictated on ground MediCtrl works on healthcare parameters & patient experience ..

What does fixed price package include?

The fixed price package typically covers all costs associated with the surgery. This typically includes: i) Hospital admission charges. ii) Surgery charges including surgeon fees, anesthetist fees and OT charges. iii) Hospital stays. iv) Cost of drugs consumed during stay at hospital

Does MediCtrl provide a second opinion for any disease?

Yes, you can avail second medical opinion by specialized doctors for all diseases. Our doctor tracks your pathology reports, your post-operative report, your discharge summary, and guides you with the required/ new treatment course ahead.

What MediCtrl provide insurance coverage for all surgeries?

MediCtrl assists you in getting the insurance approval done within 30 minutes.. Your insurance coverage depends on the health insurance type (personal, corporate) and the terms and conditions set by the insurance provider.

What MediCtrl have its own Hospitals?

MediCtrl does have its own hospital and MediCtrl also works in association with other hospitals to provide medical facility by hospital and non medical facility by MediCtrl.
